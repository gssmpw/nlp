\section{Preliminary}

\textbf{Body Model.} The SMPL~\cite{loper2023smpl} model provides a differentiable function $V = \mathcal{M}(\theta, \beta, t)$ that outputs a posed 3D mesh with $N=6890$ vertices. The pose parameter $\theta \in \mathbb{R}^{24 \times 6}$ includes a $\mathbb{R}^6$ global body rotation and the relative rotation of 23 joints with respect to their parents in a 6-D representation~\cite{zhou2019continuity}. The shape parameter $\theta \in \mathbb{R}^{10}$ represents the physique of the body shape. And $t \in \mathbb{R}^3$ means the root translation w.r.t the world coordinate.
