\section{Related Work}
In recent years, several studies have focused on the automated segmentation of bone structures.
Besler~\textit{et al}. **Wang, "Automated Proximal Femur Segmentation using Hessian-based Filtering and Graph Cut"** proposed an enhance-and-segment pipeline, which combined Hessian-based filtering and graph cut segmentation for proximal femur segmentation in CT.
Gao~\textit{et al}. **Li, "Multi-Angle Projection Network for Rib Fracture Segmentation in CT"** introduced a multi-angle projection network for rib fracture segmentation in CT, utilizing rib extraction, fracture segmentation, and multi-angle projection fusion modules to capture rib and fracture features.
Liu~\textit{et al}. **Chen, "Two-Stage Approach for Pelvic Fracture Segmentation using 3D Convolutional Neural Network"** developed a two-stage approach for pelvic fracture segmentation, using a pelvic bone segmentation network followed by a fracture segmentation network.
Cai~\textit{et al}. **Zhang, "3D U-Net-based Method for Knee CT Segmentation and Schatzker Classification"** proposed a 3D U-Net-based method for knee CT segmentation, generating 3D fracture maps and aiding Schatzker classification to improve clinical diagnostic efficiency.
Zhou~\textit{et al}. **Wang, "Framework for Hip Fracture Segmentation using Cross-Scale Attention Mechanism"** designed a framework for hip fracture segmentation, incorporating a cross-scale attention mechanism and a surface supervision strategy to enhance fracture representation and boundary accuracy.

Some studies have focused on semi-supervised segmentation of bone structures.
Zhao~\textit{et al}. **Liu, "Self-Training Method for Finger Bone Segmentation in Hand X-rays using Conditional Random Field"** proposed a self-training method for finger bone segmentation in hand X-rays, utilizing a U-Net model with a conditional random field module to generate pseudo-labels for unlabeled images.
Burton~\textit{et al}. **Xu, "Teacher-Student Model for Knee MRI Segmentation using Monte Carlo Patch Sampling"** introduced a teacher-student model for knee MRI segmentation, employing a Monte Carlo patch sampling strategy to improve accuracy.
Liu~\textit{et al}. **Chen, "Generative Adversarial Network-based Methods for Lumbosacral Structure Segmentation on Thin-Layer CT"** explored generative adversarial network (GAN)-based methods for lumbosacral structure segmentation on thin-layer CT, using semi-cGAN and few-shot-GAN models.
Li~\textit{et al}. **Zhang, "Patch-Shuffle-Based Augmentation Technique for Bone Structure Segmentation in CT"** proposed a patch-shuffle-based augmentation technique, leveraging bone structure uniqueness and consistency loss to align features between original and shuffled CT slices.
Li~\textit{et al}. **Wang, "Semi-Supervised Tooth Segmentation Method combining Entropy-Guided Mean-Teacher Approach and Weakly Mutual Consistency Network"** developed a semi-supervised tooth segmentation method combining an entropy-guided mean-teacher approach and a weakly mutual consistency network.
