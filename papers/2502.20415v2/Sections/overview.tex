\section{Overview of the existing NMAs}
\label{sec:overview}
In this section, we present the NMAs arranged in classes as defined in the Taxonomy. As it can be seen in Figure \ref{fig:taxonomy_venn}, the Classes 0-3 possess at most 1 of the 3 Traits described in Section \ref{sec:taxonomy}, while Classes 4-7 possess 2 or all of them. Every subsection consists of a small introduction listing the features common for the appropriate Class an summaries of the surveyed architectures showing key design decisions that distinguish them from other architectures. The aforementioned subsections are divided into paragraphs for three time periods: \textbf{(i)} early implementations (1998--2009), \textbf{(ii)} early modern implementations (2010--2019) and \textbf{(iii)} modern implementations (2020--2024).
