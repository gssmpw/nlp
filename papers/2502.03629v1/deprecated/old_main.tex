\begin{abstract}
Text-guided image editing datasets are synthetic which introduces bias in what types of images and requests are generated, as well as does not have human quality inputs and outputs. We propose an image editing dataset based on real human data from Reddit. We analyze the image content and instructions and compare these with existing datasets. We publish training and test sets. We evaluate SOTA models on our test set to highlight the difference between model generations and human-edited outputs. We fine tune InstructPix2Pix on our data and measure its performance.
