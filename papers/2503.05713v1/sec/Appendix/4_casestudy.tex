\section{Case Study: Prompting Without Language Instruction}
\label{tab:appendixcase}
We provide a case study where we omit the language instruction of "Answer in [LANGUAGE]" in the prompt to study the impact of this instruction. As stated in Section \ref{appendixa}, leaving out language instruction results in translation of lyrics for most models, so in this case we focus on the impact on refusal rate. We can see from Table \ref{tab:case_study} that except GPT-3.5-Turbo, language instruction is not the main contributor of the bias. In the case of GPT-3.5-Turbo, the language instruction does have an impact on refusal rate. But still, without the language instruction, GPT-3.5-Turbo has a bias in Korean and Chinese (see Table \ref{tab:case_study2}).
