\new{Due to the nature of SISA, we need to aggregate the predictions of different models. Here we tested 2 aggregation strategies on 4 datasets respectively, the results can be found in Figures~\ref{fig:aggregation_strategy1} and~\ref{fig:aggregation_strategy2}.}
