\section{Simplest problem}

In deep learning literature significant amount of work had been devoted to study the convergence of linear network. Can we get some design principles from this case for the AMOO setting?


Consider having a linear DNN of the two layers $\W\in \mathbb{R}^{1\times 2}$:
\begin{align*}
    \W = \W_1 \W_2 = a\cdot (z_1, z_2)^T = (a\cdot z_1, a\cdot z_2)
\end{align*}
Assume there are two loss functions $l_1,l_2\in \mathbb{R}^2\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$
\begin{align*}
    &l_1= \z H_1 \z = z_1^2 + \Delta z_2^2\\
    &l_2= \z H_2 \z = \Delta z_2^2 + z_1^2.
\end{align*}

Replacing $\z$ with the output of the network we get:
\begin{align*}
   &l_1= a^2 z_1^2 + \Delta a^2 z_2^2\\
   &l_2= \Delta a^2 z_1^2 +  a^2 z_2^2.
