To further support our findings, we provide a detailed one-on-one comparison of the MoENAS-S model against 10 state-of-the-art (SOTA) models as shown in figure \ref{fig:one-one}. The MoENAS-S model consistently outperformed each of the 10 SOTA models in terms of accuracy for all 10 skin tones. Also, MoENAS-S model achieved a gap of only 5.6\%, while the lowest gap achieved by the SOTA models is 9.2\%. This substantial reduction highlights our model's effectiveness in addressing disparities across different skin tones and therefore reducing bias.
