\section*{Impact Statement}
This paper investigates vulnerabilities in large language models (LLMs) within human-LLM interactions to improve safety measures in current alignment practices.
By highlighting these vulnerabilities in widely deployed LLMs, we aim to encourage protective measures before such weaknesses can be exploited at scale.
While our work identifies potential weaknesses that could be exploited, we believe transparent research on these issues is crucial for developing more robust safeguards.
