\section{Generation Accuracies for 2-Operand, 3-Digit Addition}
\label{appendix:B}
We show the generation accuracy of the full result $S$ and the digit-wise accuracy of $s_2$, compared across the different carry bit datasets, as referenced in Section \ref{subsec:digit10}.
Table \ref{tab:accuracy_2-op} shows that Gemma and Mistral struggle with the generation of the correct result digit $s_2$, exactly in the datasets that \textbf{H1} predicts to be difficult. DS3 and DS5 contain addition tasks in which a lookahead of one digit is insufficient ot determine the value of $s_2$.
